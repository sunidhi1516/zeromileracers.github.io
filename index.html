ABOUT US


We are the students of S.B. JAIN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, MANAGEMENT AND RESEARCH(SBJIT) participating in an Asia level championship ESVC-21. We were the first team from Vidharbha to participate in a Asia level event ESVC. We have been participating in these events from 4 years. The First Solar Car of our college was made in year 2016 under the guidance of Prof. V. P. KATEKAR and from that onwards our journey begins and then the Team Zero Mile Racers came into existence and this was the team who made first solar car in our college. The main motive of our team is to participate in Asia biggest event ESVC and SUVC and to gain the depth knowledge about solar cars .

Be a part of the solution, not pollution. Start your Electric vehicle journey with us, SUVC team is dedicated to provide you best guidance, technical and placement support for a bright career.

ABOUT SUVC


SUVC is a solar electric vehicle competition organised by REFRANGIBLE SOCIETY (RSTE), that challenge engineering students to work on electric vehicles with utilization of sustainable energy. SUVC known for its USP - Mentoring teams entire year with technical and non technical support by the collaboration of various automobiles and renewable energy researchers.

Saur Urja Vehicle Challenge is renowned name for E- vehicles competition. 1st season of suvc was conducted in Gwalior, Madhya pradesh with support of M.P. government while 2nd and 3rd season has conducted in coimbatore, TamilNadu with collaboration of hindustan charitable education trust.


VISION


To bring the focus of the future leaders towards Sustainable development, Cutting edge Technology & Methods to utilize green and clean energy by acting as a bridge between college world and corporate world.


MISSION


To develop students for industry-fit technocrats driven by providing conducive environment for creative and innovative education through project-based learning, case studies, frameworks.


GOAL


To apply student skills outside of the classroom by developing and design a electric -solar car that hone your Leadership Skills, Management, Interpersonal skills and develop holistically among participants.
